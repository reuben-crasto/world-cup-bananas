Build your bracket. Outsmart your mates.

World Cup Bananas is a football prediction game. Fill in your tournament predictions — group results, knockout teams, finalists and the champion — before kickoff, then earn points based on how close your calls are to the real thing.

Step by step

How brackets can be filled

1

Manual entry

Fill in match predictions for every group-stage game, yourself, one fixture at a time.

2

Score predictions

Enter expected goals for both teams. Your scores decide wins, draws, losses and the standings.

3

Knockout picks

After the groups, select who advances through each knockout round to the final.

4

Final winner

Complete the bracket by choosing the third-place winner, the two finalists, and the champion.

Scoring

Points system

The closer your call, the more you bank. Points accrue automatically as real results come in.
