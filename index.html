About the Platform

  • 🍌

    World Cup Bananas is a football prediction game where users fill in their tournament predictions.

  • 🍌

    You will predict group stage results, knockout teams, third place, finalist teams, and the final winner.

  • 🍌

    All predictions should be completed before the tournament begins or before the chosen deadline.

  • 🍌

    Points are awarded based on how close your predictions are to the actual tournament results.

How Brackets Can Be Filled

1. Manual Entry

Users can manually fill in match predictions for every group stage game.

2. Score Predictions

Enter the expected goals for both teams. These scores will decide wins, losses, draws, and standings.

3. Knockout Picks

After group predictions, users will select who advances through each knockout round.

4. Final Winner

Complete the bracket by choosing the third-place winner, finalist teams, and overall champion.

Points System

A. Group Stage

  • Correct result — win, loss, or draw: 3 points
  • Correct number of goals for one team: 1 point
  • Correct goal difference, but wrong direction: 1 point
  • Correct goal difference and correct direction: 2 points

B. Knockout Stage

  • Each correct team in the last 16: 3 points
  • Each correct team in the quarter-finals: 4 points
  • Each correct team in the semi-finals: 5 points
  • Each correct team in third place: 6 points
  • Each correct team in the final: 8 points
  • Correct third-place winner: 10 points
  • Correct tournament winner: 15 points

C. Bonus

  • Watching or participating in a selected match event: 1 point
